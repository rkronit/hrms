What happens when I update may email address(or mobile number)?

Your login email id (or mobile number)changes,likewise. You will receive all your account related communication on your

updated email address (or mobile number).


When will my Shoe4you account be updated with the new email address (or mobile number)?

It happens as soon as you confirm the varification code sent to your email (or mobile) and save the changes.


What happens to my existing Shoe4you account when I update my email address?

Updating your email address (or mobile number) doesn't invalidate your account. Your account remains fully functional you will

continue seeing your order history,saved information and personal details.


Does my Seller account get affected when I update my email address?

Shoe4you has single-sign on policy. Any changes will reflect in your Seller account also.
